Our Story

Founded by nurses. Empowered by patients.

H.E.R. Home Care was founded with a clear purpose: to fill a critical gap in home-based healthcare for Georgia families. We saw individuals who needed quality care but wanted to remain in their own homes — and we answered that call.

As a Medicaid-approved provider, we deliver skilled nursing, personal support, and respite care through three specialized waiver programs: GAPP, NOW/COMP, and EDWP. Every service we offer is rooted in our commitment to independence, dignity, and compassionate care.

Meet Our Founder

Yassmeen Singleton

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Yassmeen Singleton founded H.E.R. Home Care with a vision rooted in personal experience and professional expertise. Having witnessed firsthand the challenges families face navigating home care services, she committed herself to building an agency that places the individual — not the system — at the center of care.

Under her leadership, H.E.R. Home Care has grown into a trusted provider of Georgia Medicaid waiver services, serving families across the state with unwavering dedication.
