About us

We are here to help individuals and families thrive at home

H.E.R. Home Care is a licensed Georgia home care agency dedicated to delivering high-quality, person-centered services. We empower individuals of all ages to live independently and with dignity in the comfort of their own homes.

Our team of compassionate caregivers provides skilled nursing, personal support, and respite care through Georgia Medicaid waiver programs—ensuring every client receives the individualized attention they deserve.

Know more about us

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional care that allows our clients to live with comfort, independence, and dignity in the place they call home.

Founded by Nurses. Empowered by Patients.

Our Services

Designed to support independence and well-being

Personal Care

Assistance with activities of daily living including bathing, grooming, dressing, and mobility support to help clients maintain their independence and dignity at home

Skilled Nursing

Registered and licensed practical nurses providing medication management, wound care, vital sign monitoring, and specialized health assessments in the comfort of home

Companion Care

Meaningful social engagement, light housekeeping, meal preparation, errands, and transportation to appointments to combat isolation and improve quality of life

Respite Care

Temporary relief for primary family caregivers, allowing them to rest and recharge while their loved one receives attentive, professional care in a familiar environment

Health Monitoring

Ongoing wellness checks, medication reminders, chronic condition management, and coordination with physicians to ensure optimal health outcomes for every client

Community Integration

Support with participating in community activities, social programs, and day-to-day outings that promote social inclusion, well-being, and an enriched quality of life
