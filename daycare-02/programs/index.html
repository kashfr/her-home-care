Our Programs

Specialized waivers designed for your family's needs

We offer three Medicaid waiver programs, each tailored to a specific population. Our team guides you through eligibility, enrollment, and every step of care — at no cost to you.

01
Georgia Pediatric Program

GAPP Waiver

Providing in-home skilled nursing and personal care services to medically fragile children under 21 years of age. Our dedicated pediatric nurses partner with families to keep children healthy, comfortable, and safe — without a hospital stay.

  • Medically Fragile Children (Under 21)
  • Skilled Nursing & Personal Care
  • In-Home Medical Support
Apply for GAPP →
02
Intellectual & Developmental Disabilities

NOW / COMP Waiver

Offering comprehensive support and services for adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD). We help individuals live more independently and stay active members of their community with dignity and respect.

  • Intellectual Disabilities (IDD)
  • Developmental Disabilities
  • Community & In-Home Support
Apply for NOW/COMP →
03
Elderly & Disabled Waiver Program

EDWP Waiver

Providing community and in-home services to elderly and physically disabled adults so they can remain in their own homes. Our caregivers assist with daily activities, personal care, and medical needs to support safe, independent living.

  • Elderly Adults & Seniors
  • Physical Disabilities
  • In-Home Personal Care
Apply for EDWP →
